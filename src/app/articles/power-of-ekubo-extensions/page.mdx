Ekubo is a next-generation decentralized exchange (DEX) on Starknet built by Ekubo Inc. and managed by a DAO. It is one of the most advanced automated market maker (AMM) on the blockchain, offering a wide range of features like DCA orders,  concentrated liquidity, and most especially till pattern and extendability with extensions.

## What are Ekubo Extensions
> Extensions allow you to insert custom logic at certain pool lifecycle events. Extensions can also re-enter core contracts (as can any other contract) in order to place their own swaps, updates, etc. before or after the external contract interacts with a pool. This enables third party developers to leverage the efficient and secure core AMM protocol of Ekubo, including concentrated liquidity, without implementing any of the maths.

## Key Advantages of Ekubo Extensions
1. **Tailored Pool Behavior**: Developers can fine-tune pool operations to meet specific trading strategies, risk management requirements or market needs.
2. **Isolated Risk**: Since each pool maintains separate state, capital in pools are isolated from each other, reducing the risk of a single pool failure affecting the entire system.
3. **Efficient Core Protocol Utilization**: Extensions leverage Ekubo's robust core AMM protocol, including concentrated liquidity, without reimplementing complex mathematics.
